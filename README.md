# Automotive Lead Conversion Analytics

## Problem Statement
A multi-city automotive dealership lacked structured reporting on 
lead conversion funnels, TAT benchmarks, and store-level NPS performance.

## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel | Pivot Tables | Advanced Formulas | Statistical Analysis | MIS Reporting

## Dataset
- 1,000 lead records across 4 metro stores
- Stores: Bangalore MBC, Chennai MBC, Delhi MBC, Mumbai MBC

## Key Findings
- Overall lead-to-delivery conversion rate: 81.2%
- Average TAT: Pre-Booking 3.06 days | Booking 3.07 days | Delivery 4.46 days
- "Price" was the top cancellation driver at 22.5% of all cancelled leads
- Lead split: Hot 30.3% | Warm 50.9% | Cold 18.8%
- Chennai MBC had the highest NPS average (5.01)

## Business Impact
- TAT benchmarking surfaced operational bottlenecks per store
- Cancellation analysis enabled targeted sales intervention strategies
- NPS scorecards and lead segmentation supported data-driven prioritization
